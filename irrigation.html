💧 Irrigation Guide

Maharashtra crop watering schedule

🧅 Onion

Water every 6–8 days in winter

Water every 4–5 days in summer

Avoid waterlogging

🍅 Tomato

Water every 5–6 days

Maintain moist soil

Drip irrigation recommended

🌶 Chilli

Water every 6–7 days

Avoid excess moisture

🥔 Potato

Water every 7 days

Increase watering during tuber formation

🥬 Cabbage

Water every 5–6 days

Keep soil consistently moist

🌱 Spinach

Light watering every 3–4 days

Shallow root crop
