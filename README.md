<h2>📖 About the Project</h2>

<p>
Agriculture plays a vital role in the economy, but farmers often face challenges due to
changing weather conditions, lack of timely information, and difficulty accessing
reliable farming resources.
</p>

<p>
<b>KrushiCare</b> is a smart agriculture platform developed to help farmers make better
farming decisions by providing real-time weather updates, crop recommendations,
and useful agricultural information through a simple and user-friendly web interface.
</p>

<p>
The platform aims to reduce uncertainty in farming by delivering accurate weather
information and helping farmers choose suitable crops based on seasonal conditions.
</p>

<hr>

<h2>⚙️ How the Platform Works</h2>

<p>
Users can access the platform through a web browser without installing any software.
</p>

<p>
After opening the application, farmers can view weather information for their location,
explore crop recommendations, and access useful farming guidance from a centralized dashboard.
</p>

<p>
The platform retrieves live weather data through API integration and displays it in an
easy-to-understand format, allowing users to plan irrigation, cultivation, and harvesting activities more efficiently.
</p>

<p>
The responsive design ensures the application works smoothly across desktops,
tablets, and mobile devices.
</p>
